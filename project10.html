Our Idea

Water Alarming System

The project aims to create a reliable and cost-effective system that alerts users when water levels reach critical points, preventing wastage or overflow.

Material Required

  • Cardboard: For construction
  • Buzzer: For sound
  • Battery: For energy
  • Switch: For closed and open circuit
  • Plastic container: For testing purpose

Costing

  • Buzzer: ₹15
  • Battery & Wire: ₹15
  • Total: ₹30

Process

  • Assemble the circuit
  • Connect the Buzzer, Battery, and Switch.

Implications

  • Water Conservation: Prevents water wastage by alerting when the tank is full.
  • Household Utility: Simple yet effective for homes, schools, and offices.
  • Scalability: Can be used in agricultural fields for efficient irrigation management.

Future Scope

  • Automation Integration: Linking with smart home systems for automatic water control.
  • Wireless Connectivity: Using IoT to send alerts to mobile devices.
  • Advanced Sensors: Experimenting with more accurate and durable sensors.
