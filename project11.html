Our Idea

Buzzer Wire Game: A fun and engaging skill test.

Develop and test your hand-eye coordination, the challenge lies in guiding a handled loop along a wired path without triggering the buzzers.

Materials Required

  • G.I. Wire: To form the challenging path.
  • Battery and Buzzer: To produce sound when the wire is toucehd.
  • Plastic Pipe: For making an insulated handle.
  • Cardboard: To create a base structure.

Costing

  • GI wire: almost free
  • Buzzer: ₹15
  • Battery: ₹20
  • Cardboard: almost free
  • Total: ₹35
