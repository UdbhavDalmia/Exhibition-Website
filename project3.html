Our Idea

Eco Brick

To create Ecobricks from used plastic packets, demonstrating a sustainable way to manage non-recyclable waste by repurposing it into construction material for community projects.

Materials Required

  • Plastic packets, empty plastic bottles, plastic sticks, and gloves (for hygiene)

Costing

  • Minimal, as the primary materials are waste products

Process

  • Collect Plastic Waste: Clean and dry plastic packets
  • Pack Tightly: Pack plastic packets tightly into empty plastic bottles.
  • Seal & Label: Seal and label the Ecobricks.

Implications

  • Environmental Impact: Reduces plastic waste pollution and promotes responsible waste management practices.
  • Social Impact: Can be used in community projects, such as building playgrounds or school furniture, benefiting society and promoting environmental awareness.

Future Scope

  • Expanding the Ecobrick project to involve more community members.
  • Partnering with local organizations to use Ecobricks in larger construction projects.
  • Raising awareness through workshops and educational campaigns on the importance of recycling and reducing plastic waste.
