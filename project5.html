Our Idea

Multitasking Pen Holder: Organize, Store, and Decorate Efficiently.

The objective of this project is to create a multi-functional pen holder that not only organizes stationery items but also adds an aesthetic touch to your study. It maximizes the use of space and utilizes recyclable materials for an eco-friendly approach.

Materials Required

  • Cardboard: To create the base and compartments of the holder.
  • Reusable plastic and popsicle sticks: For additional support and decoration.
  • Colored paper and decorative elements: To make it visually appealing.
  • Glue and tape: For assembly.
  • Paint and brushes: For final touches and customization.

Costing

  • Cardboard and popsicle sticks: ₹20
  • Colored paper and decorative materials: ₹10
  • Glue and paint: ₹20
  • Total Cost: ₹50

Process

  • Design Layout: Sketch the design, including compartments for pens, pencils, and additional storage for items like paper clips and sticky notes.
  • Cut and Assemble: Cut the cardboard pieces to size and assemble them using glue to form the base structure.
  • Add Compartments: Attach smaller boxes and use popsicle sticks to create sections for separating items.
  • Decorate: Cover the pen holder with colored paper and decorate it with flowers, paint, or other accessories.
  • Finishing Touches: Ensure all compartments are sturdy and functional. Check everything before use.

Implications

  • Organization and Accessibility: Keeps stationery items neatly arranged, saving time and effort.
  • Eco-Friendly Initiative: Promotes recycling and creative reuse of materials.
  • Aesthetic Addition: Enhances the look of your workspace with its colorful and artistic design.

Future Scope

  • Customizable Designs: Exploring designs tailored to specific user needs and workspace themes.
  • Material Innovation: Using more durable or biodegradable materials for a long-lasting, eco-friendly solution.
  • Community Workshops: Teaching others how to create these holders to encourage upcycling and environmental awareness.
