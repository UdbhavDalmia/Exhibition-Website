Our Idea

Eco Friendly Raincoat and Handbag: A step to sustainable fashion

Explore the innovative concept of fashion by reusing waste materials. This groundbreaking method has immense potential for creating a new trend in fashion.

Materials Required

  • Used milk packets or other plastic packets.

Costing

  • As it is made of used materials, the cost is negligible.

Process

  • Setup: Rinse and clean the packets properly.
  • Design: Make the layout of the design of your choice.
  • Dry and Sew: Dry the cleaned packets and Sew them according to your design layout.

Implications

  • Waste Reduction: Converts non-biodegradeable waste into useful items.
  • Promotes Awareness: Encourages sustainable practices and creative recycling.

Future Scope

  • Material Durability: Investigating methods to make the plastic more stronger and long lasting.
  • Fashion innovation: Exploring new design ideas to make sustainable fashion more mainstream.
  • Efficiency Improvements: Increase transfer efficiency over longer distances.
