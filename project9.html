Our Idea

Vacuum Cleaner

Create an efficient and functional vacuum cleaner using readily available waste materials, demonstrating how everyday trash can be repurposed into useful gadgets that benefit both households and the environment

Materials Required

  • Plastic Pipe
  • Cardboard
  • Fan Switcher
  • Battery

Costing

  • Motor: ₹15
  • Other components were repurposed from available waste materials, making this a highly cost-effective and sustainable project.
  • Battery: ₹15
  • Total: ₹30

Process

  • Plastic bottle body: Used as the main chamber to house the vacuuming mechanism and collect waste.
  • Motor and battery setup: Connected to a simple on/off switch to control the vacuum. The motor generates suction to collect waste effectively.

Implications

  • The project highlights the potential of waste management through innovative reuse, reducing plastic waste, and raising awareness about sustainable practices among students and the community.

Future Scope

  • Incorporating solar-powered energy to make the lamp more eco-friendly.
  • Enhancing the design for increased durability and aesthetic appeal.
  • Using smart lighting technology to make it more energy-efficient.
